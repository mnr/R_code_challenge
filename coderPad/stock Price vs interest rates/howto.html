Find the Correlation between Stock Price and Interest Rates

I’ve supplied you with two data sets:
  • AAPL stock prices from June 6, 2019 to the current day, a data.frame titled AAPL
    • The AAPL data.frame contains these variables:
      • Date
      • Open
      • High
      • Low
      • Close
      • Adj.Close
      • Volume
  • US Interest Rates from July 1954 to the current day, a data.frame titled interestRates
    • The interestRates data.frame contains these variables:
      • date
      • value
Your task: Return the correlation between the daily closing price (AAPL$Close) and the interest rate interestRates$date for a given range of dates.

Parameters

firstDate: The first date of a range of dates as a string in YYYY-MM-DD format

lastDate: The last date of a range of dates as a string in YYYY-MM-DD format

Result

float: The correlation between daily closing price and the daily interest rate. A number between 1 and -1.

Example 1:

Input: firstDate = “2019-06-06”, lastDate = “2021-06-06" 
Result: -0.7971276

Example 2:

Input: firstDate = “2019-06-06”, lastDate = “2024-06-06" 
Result: 0.4744185
