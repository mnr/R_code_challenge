Join Three Datasets

Data scientists spend about 80% of their time cleaning and preparing data. I've also heard that data scientists spend an additional 10% of their time complaining about cleaning and preparing data. So the more proficient you are at cleaning data, the better data scientist you'll be.
 
Your task:
In this challenge, you're provided with three data.frames from two different survey companies. The first two data sets are from Acme Data.  The third set that you'll obtain is from Star Research. They combined demographic and survey data, but used different column headers than those used by Acme. 
  • AcmeData_Demographic and AcmeData_Survey - For privacy concerns, Acme separated demographic information from the survey results.
  • StarResearch - Combined demographic and survey data, but with different column headers than those used by Acme.

Combine all three of these data frames, then for each house color, identify the individual with the highest income. Return this as a data.frame in the following format:

firstName Income houseColor
Hallie 50563.2 Blue
Natavia 68595.0 Gray
Jalal 10654.3 Red

Parameters

None:  

Result

data.frame: a table showing the individual with the maximum income for each house color.
